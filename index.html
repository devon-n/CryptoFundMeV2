How it Works

Mint

The picture and text of from your fund is stored on the blockchain as NFTs. This NFT is minted to your chain address.

That means only you own it and no one can censor you.

Donate

People can donate to your NFT through either through this website or any other way they access the blockchain network.

That means no one can force us to stop people from donating to you.

Withdraw

You can withdraw your funds at any point to your address and can even transfer the fund to a different owner.

That means no one can stop you from getting your funds when you need them.

Immutable

Anyone can create, donate and withdraw even if they cannot access this website. They can do it through a block explorer (etherscan.io) or connect directly with a script.

Transferable

You can transfer these funds from account to account and only the account holder can withdraw funds but people can still donate.
